The Venue

Where?

We're getting married in Golden, Colorado. This is about a 40 minute drive from the Denver Airport (DIA). The adress is: 403 Blue Ridge Road, Golden, CO 80401.

Getting there

There is a busline that stops a 10 minute walk from the venue, and the venue is accesible by both Lyft and Uber.

Hotels

Look at Booking.com or Airbnb for current options. There should be plenty available nearby. The venue is 30 minutes from Denver.

Parking

There is limited parking available on the venue, so carpooling is highly recommended. Contact us if you come by RV. There is also a huge free overnight parking lot nearby called the Wooly Mammoth Park-N-Ride, from which we can pick you up.

Camping

Can I camp on the venue? Yes, you can! There is plenty of space available for you to pitch your tent and we should have room for some RV's. Please contact us if your planning on camping here, so we know how many people to expect. We have the place from the 28th of July at 4 pm until the 31st at 10 am. You're welcome to camp for the extra days. You have to bring your own food and drinks for the non-wedding days. But there are four big grills to heat any food on. Please mind that we have to be out at 10.00 on Sunday and the place has to be tidy.
